If you would ask me what my biggest accomplishement was, I would maybe surprise you. My boss once asked me to come up with a new corporate tool to support the entire global sales teams. A hyper modern sales tool that gave the sales teams, all the necessary information to perform at better their task. I got 3 months to come up with a prototype and roadmap

Instead of looking how other competitors used complex systems to provide realtime information through smart algorithms. And doing months of discovery. I asked a select group of sales reps, what would be the biggest support to have when conducting sales. Their answers were simple. I have to find the right info as quick as possible.
